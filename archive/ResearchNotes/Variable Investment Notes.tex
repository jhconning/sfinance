\subsection{Variable Investment case}

$pXI\geq \frac{p}{\Delta }B(m)I-A+\gamma (I-I_{m})+\frac{p}{\Delta }mI$

$\beta I_{m}=\frac{p}{\Delta }mI-mI-F$

$A^{\prime }(X,m)=\frac{p}{\Delta }B(m)I-\left[ pXI-\gamma I\right] -\frac{%
\gamma }{\beta }\left[ \frac{p}{\Delta }mI-mI-F\right] +\frac{p}{\Delta }mI$

if $\beta =\gamma $

$A(X,m,I)=\frac{p}{\Delta }B(m)I-\left[ pXI-\gamma I\right] +mI+F$

When $\beta >\gamma $

$A^{\prime }(X,m)=\frac{p}{\Delta }B(m)I-\left[ pXI-\gamma I\right] -\frac{%
\gamma }{\beta }\left[ \frac{p}{\Delta }mI-mI-F\right] +\frac{p}{\Delta }mI$

$A^{\prime }(X,m)=\left[ A(X,m,I)-mI-F\right] -\frac{\gamma }{\beta }\left[ 
\frac{p}{\Delta }mI-mI-F\right] +\frac{p}{\Delta }mI$

$A^{\prime }(X,m)=A(X,m,I)+\frac{\beta -\gamma }{\beta }\left[ \frac{p}{%
\Delta }mI-mI-F\right] $

$A^{\prime }(X,m)=A(X,m,I)+(\beta -\gamma )I_{m}$

If $m=0$

$A(X,0,I)=\frac{p}{\Delta }B_{0}I-\left[ pXI-\gamma I\right] +F$

max loan size for borrower with assets $A$ would be:

$A=\frac{p}{\Delta }B_{0}I-\left[ pXI-\gamma I\right] +F$

$I=\frac{A-F}{\left[ \frac{p}{\Delta }B_{0}-\left[ pX-\gamma \right] \right] 
}$

Suppose allow monitoring at level \ $m_{1}$

($\beta =\gamma $ case)

$A(X,m_{1},I)=\frac{p}{\Delta }B(m_{1})I-\left[ pXI-\gamma I\right] +m_{1}I+F
$

$A=\frac{p}{\Delta }B(m_{1})I-\left[ pXI-\gamma I\right] +m_{1}I+F$

$I=\frac{A-F}{\left[ \frac{p}{\Delta }B(m_{1})-\left[ pX-\gamma \right]
+m_{1}\right] }$\newline
\newline
by assumption $\frac{p}{\Delta }B^{\prime }(m_{1})+1<0$ so monitoring lowers
collateral req and increases loan size.

Note that in the above it follows that

$I_{m}=\frac{1}{\beta }\left[ \frac{p}{\Delta }mI-mI-F\right] $

$=-\frac{F}{\beta }+\frac{q}{\Delta }mI$

$=-\frac{F}{\beta }+\frac{q}{\Delta }m_{1}\frac{\left[ A-F\right] }{\left[ 
\frac{p}{\Delta }B(m_{1})-\left[ pX-\gamma \right] +m_{1}\right] }$ \ which
is increasing in $m$

But does borrower want higher monitoring?

Borrower earns:

$pXI-\gamma (I-I_{m})-\beta I_{m}-F$

$=pXI-\gamma I-(\beta -\gamma )I_{m}-F$

$=\left[ pX-\gamma \right] I-\frac{(\beta -\gamma )}{\beta }\left[ \frac{p}{%
\Delta }mI-mI-F\right] $

$=\left[ \left[ pX-\gamma \right] -\frac{(\beta -\gamma )}{\beta }\frac{q}{%
\Delta }m]\right] I+\frac{(\beta -\gamma )}{\beta }F$

$\left[ \left[ pX-\gamma \right] -\frac{(\beta -\gamma )}{\beta }\frac{q}{%
\Delta }m]\right] \frac{\left[ A-F\right] }{\left[ \frac{p}{\Delta }B(m_{1})-%
\left[ pX-\gamma \right] +m_{1}\right] }+\frac{(\beta -\gamma )}{\beta }F$

in $\beta =\gamma $

$\left[ \left[ pX-\gamma \right] ]\right] \frac{\left[ A-F\right] }{\left[ 
\frac{p}{\Delta }B(m_{1})-\left[ pX-\gamma \right] +m_{1}\right] }$

which is decreasing in $m,$ so borrower does not want monitoring, unless
necessary...
